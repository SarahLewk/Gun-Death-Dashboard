Gun Deaths in America: Let's Explore...



"Gun Deaths in America" examines data from various sources such as (CDC) The Center for Disease Control, Kaggle, GSS Data Explorer, FBI Uniform Crime Report, (ATF)The Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives, and the US Census Bureau .

Explore this site to get an inside look into what accounts for the most gun related deaths in America. There are many questions to be answered such as: "Do certain socio-economic factors play into gun violence?", "Has gun ownership increased?", and "What was the intent?"



With the use of matplotlib, I am able to provide some informative visualizations to help translate the raw data.
This information can be interpreted in many ways. Some will see this data and think that guns are not the problem, and that there are other circumstances leading up to these tragic outcomes such as mental health, but are they right? Take a look at these visualizations and draw your own conclusion.
