Gun Violence With Intent

Sadly suicide is the leading cause of gun deaths. Which brings up the question, “Why do so many Americans choose to end their life?” For many this calls attention to the need for better mental health care and suicide prevention methods rather than gun control. There is certainly more data that can be explored such as how many suicides are actually carried out by a gun versus other means. Mental health is tricky though and unfortunately a mental illness can go undetected until something tragic happens.

Mental State

Suicide is often an impulsive act and it’s possible guns may lead to a more quick and lethal outcome than other means of suicide. Restricting access to guns may perhaps give someone more time to think about the severity of those actions before carrying it out. If doing this saves just one life....wouldn't it be worth it?
